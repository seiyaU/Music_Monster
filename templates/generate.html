About This App

The goal of this app is to provide original monster cards based on each user's music data.

“Listen to good music to get good monsters.”

Currently, it only uses my own Spotify history, even if you log in — so please enjoy it as a simple monster card generator for now.

Each card has a unique ID, and you’ll never see the same image twice.
Collect them, trade them, and battle with your friends!
